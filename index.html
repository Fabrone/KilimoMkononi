Kilimo Mkononi Data Privacy Policy Effective Date: 23rd, July, 2025 At Kilimo Mkononi, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Data Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application, Kilimo Mkononi, designed to assist farmers with weather forecasts, pest and disease identification, data storage, and more. By accessing and using Kilimo Mkononi, you agree to the terms and conditions of this Privacy Policy. 1. Information We Collect We collect different types of information when you use Kilimo Mkononi, which can be broadly categorized as follows: a. Personal Information: This includes information that can identify you personally, such as: • Name (if you provide it) • Email address (if you create an account or subscribe to notifications) • Phone number (optional for account verification or support) • Location data (e.g., GPS coordinates to provide location-based weather forecasts or pest data) b. Usage Data: We collect data on how you use the application, which may include: • Device Information: Type of device, operating system version, and device settings. • Usage Data: How often and for how long you use the application, the features you access, and interactions with content (weather data, pest information, etc.). • Log Data: Information such as IP addresses, browser type, operating system, pages viewed, and timestamps of app activity. c. Location Data: If you choose to enable location-based services (for weather forecasts or pest identification relevant to your geographic region), we collect your geolocation through GPS or network-based positioning. d. Agricultural Data: We may collect data related to farming practices, such as: • Crop data: Type of crops planted, planting dates, harvest dates, yield predictions. • Pest and disease reports: Information entered by you about pest outbreaks or disease symptoms. This data is used to personalize your experience and improve the services we offer. 2. How We Use Your Information We use the information we collect for various purposes, including: • Providing Services: To deliver weather forecasts, pest and disease identification, and agricultural tips based on your location and farming needs. • Improving the App: To monitor and enhance the performance of the app, fix bugs, and improve user experience. • Personalization: To offer tailored content such as personalized weather updates and pest/disease identification relevant to your farming activities. • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, feedback, or troubleshooting requests. • Notifications: To send you important updates, reminders, or alerts related to weather, pest control, or other relevant information. • Marketing: (Only if you opt-in) To send promotional material or surveys related to our services (you may opt-out of marketing communications at any time). 3.
